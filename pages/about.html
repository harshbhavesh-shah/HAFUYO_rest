Our Story

Harmony, Fusion, and Global Flavors

Founded in Harmony

Founded in 2020, Hafuyo was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to celebrate the rich diversity of global cuisine in one welcoming space. Our name, "Hafuyo", represents harmony and fusion.

Located in the heart of Aix-en-Provence, we bring together the finest culinary traditions from Italy, Mexico, China, India, and France.
